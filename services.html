Complete Kitchen Setup Services

From a blank room to a fully operating professional kitchen — we handle layout planning, custom fabrication, and on-site setup.

01

Kitchen Layout Planning & CAD Design

A good commercial kitchen starts on the drawing board. Our designers create precise 2D CAD layout plans that optimize food prep workflow, ensure fire safety exits, and divide your kitchen into efficient sections (washing, prep, cooking, plating, and storage).

Work flow optimization to prevent cross-contamination Exhaust hood sizing & exhaust duct path planning Gas bank and pipeline design matching safety norms
02

Custom Equipment Fabrication

Every commercial kitchen has unique pillars, corners, or layout constraints. We custom-fabricate stainless steel tables, sinks, exhaust hoods, gas stoves, and storage cabinets to perfectly fit your available floor space without wasting any room.

Heavy-duty food-grade Stainless Steel (SS 304 / SS 202) Customized burner placements & high pressure configurations Reinforced under-shelves and sound-dampened worktops
03

Installation & Commissioning

Our experienced Lucknow installation crew delivers the equipment to your site, moves it into position, levels the tables, hooks up exhaust hoods, connects gas lines, and tests burners. We make sure everything is functioning before we leave.

Gas manifold testing for pressure leaks Burner tuning for maximum flame efficiency & blue flame output Calibration of digital thermostats on vertical chillers
04

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

A breakdown in a busy commercial kitchen can halt business. We provide structured Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) and prompt emergency repairs to keep your gas lines, stoves, grinders, and commercial refrigeration running 24/7 without issues.

Periodic cleaning & checking of gas stove burners Refrigeration compressor checking and gas top-up Special discount on spare parts replacement under AMC
