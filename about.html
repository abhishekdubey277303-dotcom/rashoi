Our Profile

Shaping the Future of Commercial Kitchens

Established in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, Rasoi Equipments has grown to become one of the region's most reputable manufacturers and suppliers of commercial kitchen equipment. We design, fabricate, and install custom solutions that cater to hotels, restaurants, industrial canteens, dhabas, cloud kitchens, and hospitals.

Our Lucknow-based manufacturing unit is equipped with advanced machinery to handle sheet metal bending, welding, and high-precision fabrication of food-grade stainless steel (SS 304 and SS 202). We are committed to hygiene, ease of cleaning, and durability in everything we build.

Whether you need a high-power Chinese cooking range, a temperature-controlled vertical chiller, or customized display racks, our engineers work alongside you to maximize your kitchen's space, hygiene, and overall efficiency.

Our Vision

To be India's leading and most trusted manufacturer of commercial kitchen systems. We aim to continually innovate in terms of energy efficiency, kitchen space management, and high-performance engineering to help culinary businesses grow seamlessly.

Our Mission

To design and fabricate high-quality, durable, and hygiene-focused stainless steel equipments tailored to our clients' precise needs. We strive to provide end-to-end setup services—from initial layout drawing to commissioning—coupled with reliable after-sales maintenance.

Premium Quality

We use high-grade stainless steel (SS 304/202) ensuring rust resistance, fire safety, and long lifecycle.

Custom Designs

Every commercial kitchen is unique. We customize sizing, stove configurations, and refrigeration units accordingly.

Direct Factory Rates

We fabricate in-house in Lucknow, which allows us to eliminate dealer margins and pass the savings to you.

AMC & Service

Our commitment doesn't end with delivery. We offer Annual Maintenance Contracts and prompt emergency repair services.
